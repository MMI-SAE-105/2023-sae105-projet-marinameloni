    • Merry Go Round of Life: The Enchanted Soundtrack of Howl's Moving Castle

    Written by Marina Meloni • 2 min 30 read

    In the enchantment of the animation world, one name stands out when talking about the harmonious marriage of sound and images: Mamoru Fujisawa, known professionally as Joe Hisaishi. His musical compositions have left an indelible mark in the cinema realm, especially in Hayao Miyazaki's works. His enchanting composition, "Merry-Go-Round of Life," present in "Howl's Moving Castle," perfectly illustrates his ability to enhance storytelling through music. Born in Nagano, Japan, Mamoru Fujisawa knew he needed to adopt a new name to make himself more accessible abroad. Thus, he chose the more accessible stage name Joe Hisaishi. His contributions to the world of film music are simply extraordinary. His collaborations with Hayao Miyazaki are particularly remarkable as his compositions played an essential role in the emotional depth and immersive quality of Miyazaki's films. "Merry-Go-Round of Life" is a testament to Hisaishi's incredible talent. This piece evokes a sense of nostalgia and transports the listener into a world of wonder, reflecting the enchanting narration of the film. This waltz, with its whimsical melody and elegant orchestration, captures the essence of fantasy and escapism, essential elements of the magic of "Howl's Moving Castle."

    Photo from the film, The character "Turnip" highlighted.

    Hisaishi's orchestrated musical journey complements the visual art and storytelling of the film, inviting viewers to escape into a world where magic, love, and adventure await them. The enchanting melody of "Merry-Go-Round of Life" serves as a gateway to the imaginative universe created by Hayao Miyazaki. Hisaishi's ability to capture the essence of the story through music is truly magical. He understands the power of a well-composed score to connect the audience with the characters and their emotional journeys. His work in "Howl's Moving Castle" and many other films has solidified his status as one of the eminent composers in the world of animation. As we delve into the enchanting world of "Howl's Moving Castle," it becomes evident that Joe Hisaishi's musical genius is an indispensable part of the storytelling. His compositions, including "Merry-Go-Round of Life," are more than just accompanying music; they are the very heart of the film.

    Photo from the film, Kingsbury city.

    With his music, Hisaishi has the remarkable ability to transport us into fantastical worlds and make us believe in the magic of cinema. The enchantment surrounding "Howl's Moving Castle" extends beyond its visual splendor, reaching into the captivating melodies that persist long after the credits roll.
